The Chemistry Conundrum

How to balance spark & substance when you’re dating at the top.

{% endblock %} {% block content %}

“We just didn’t have chemistry.” — the most common post-date verdict. But what is chemistry, really, and does it reliably predict long-term compatibility?

1 · Understand what chemistry is (and isn’t)

Neurologically, that lightning-bolt feeling is a dopamine & norepinephrine cocktail. Great for butterflies; terrible for sober judgement. …

2 · The three layers of attraction

  1. Visual spark — face symmetry, body language, grooming.
  2. Intellectual resonance — shared humour, curiosity, values.
  3. Emotional safety — ability to be seen & accepted.

3 · Re-calibrating your ‘chemistry radar’

Elite professionals often have a hyper-selective radar tuned to ‘wow’ moments. Try a “10 % rule”: if there’s even a 10 % intrigue, grant a second date…

True compatibility feels like calm excitement—not a roller-coaster.

4 · Put the science to work

  • Schedule dates to be 90 minutes max—keeps dopamine spikes manageable.
  • Swap “What do you do?” for “What lights you up outside of work?”
  • End the evening with one genuine compliment plus a concrete next step.
